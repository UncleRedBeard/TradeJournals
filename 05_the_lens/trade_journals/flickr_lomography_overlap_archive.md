# Flickr: Lomography Overlap Archive

## Purpose

Catalog Flickr albums that overlap known Lomography film catalog entries. This entry keeps the Flickr source material separate from the Lomography catalog so album URLs, photo IDs, platform metadata, and future photo review notes do not get conflated.

## Archive Context

These albums belong in `05_the_lens` because they represent film/photo bodies of work already described in the Lomography catalog, while Flickr preserves a separate public album source. The goal is not to duplicate the Lomography entries, but to make the Flickr side of the evidence discoverable and traceable.
