## Access Notes

Anonymous access to the raw album URL was blocked by Lomography's JavaScript and cookie gate, and a real browser session redirected to Lomography login. After the user logged in through the in-app browser, the album editor became accessible and showed the album as visible.

The logged-in editor view included controls for the album name, visibility, saving, deletion, and a broader photo-library picker. The public-facing album link exposed from the editor is the preferred citation URL for future journal references.
