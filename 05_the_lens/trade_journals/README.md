# Lens Trade Journals

Use this folder for photography, documentation, and visual archive journals. Entries should explain how images support the craft record and how photo evidence is organized for future portfolio use.

## Journals

- [Lomography Film Album Catalog](lomography_film_album_catalog.md) - catalog record for 35mm Lomography albums and their image metadata, with notes separating them from 120 film Isolette work.
- [Flickr: my hood](flickr_my_hood_35mm.md) - catalog record for a 35mm film Flickr album related to the lens archive.
- [Lomography: Isolette - First Shots](lomography_isolette_first_shots.md) - catalog record for the Lomography album and its logged-in access path.
- [Lomography: X-Mas 2016 Smithville -> Sacramento Road Trip](lomography_xmas_2016_road_trip.md) - catalog record for a Lomography road trip album shot with the 1961 Agfa Isolette on 120 film.
