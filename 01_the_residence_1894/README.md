# The Residence: 1894 Historic Home Restoration

Documents restoration work on the 1894 residence, with emphasis on historic preservation, structural stabilization, old-growth materials, and repair decisions that protect original building fabric.

## Suggested Journal Topics

- structural framing and sill stabilization
- foundation, piers, leveling, and deflection correction
- finish carpentry and millwork replication
- historic windows, sash repair, glazing, and wavy glass
- exterior envelope, siding, drainage, and weather protection
- modern system integration inside historic framing
