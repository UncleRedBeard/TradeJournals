# Residence Trade Journals

Use this folder for 1894 residence restoration journals. Organize entries by trade, system, room, or major intervention so each journal can explain the problem, preservation context, execution, tradeoffs, and visual evidence.

## Journals

- [Entry Restoration](entry_restoration.md) - threshold-area restoration journal for the entry work and its Flickr evidence archive.
- [Guest Bath Restoration](guest_bath_restoration.md) - room-scale restoration journal for the guest bath work and its Flickr evidence archive.
- [Landing Restoration](landing_restoration.md) - circulation-space restoration journal for the landing and landing-floor Flickr evidence archives.
- [Master Bath Restoration](master_bath_restoration.md) - room-scale restoration journal for the master bath work and its Flickr evidence archive.
- [Master Bedroom Restoration](master_bedroom_restoration.md) - room-scale restoration journal for the master bedroom work and its Flickr evidence archive.
- [Office Restoration](office_restoration.md) - room-scale restoration journal
  for the office/studio work, including Flickr evidence and the Google Photos
  `Home Reno - Studio` archive.
