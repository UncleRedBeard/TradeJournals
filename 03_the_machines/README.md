# The Machines

Documents vintage scooter, motorcycle, and mechanical restoration work, with emphasis on preservation-minded repair, careful disassembly, original material stewardship, mechanical diagnosis, and visible process evidence.

- chassis and bodywork

- fuel, carburetion, and intake
- electrical systems and lighting
- wheels, brakes, suspension, and controls
- paint, trim, hardware, and period-correct details
